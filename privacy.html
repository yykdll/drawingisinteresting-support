Privacy Policy

Introduction

Thank you for using Drawing Perfect Shapes ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By accessing or using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

Information We Collect

To provide you with the best experience and improve our services, we collect several types of information:

Drawing Data

Shapes, patterns and illustrations you create within the app

Usage Information

Progress, scores, feature interaction, and challenge completion

Device Information

Device model, OS version, screen dimensions and settings

How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect in various ways to provide, maintain and improve our services:

  • To provide and operate the drawing functionality
  • To calculate accuracy scores in real-time
  • To track and visualize your progress across different challenge levels
  • To improve the accuracy and performance of our scoring algorithms
  • To personalize challenge recommendations based on your skill level
  • To analyze app performance and crash reports for stability improvements
  • To develop new features, products and services

Important: We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. All data is used exclusively to enhance your experience within the Drawing Perfect Shapes app.

Data Storage & Security

Storage Locations

  • Primary Storage: All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework
  • iCloud Backups: If you enable iCloud synchronization, your progress will be backed up to your personal iCloud account
  • Temporary Files: Cache files stored on-device for performance optimization

Security Measures

  • Encryption: Data encryption at rest using Apple's Data Protection API
  • Authentication: Secure token-based authentication for premium content
  • Access Control: Strict access controls on our development team
  • Regular Audits: Security reviews and vulnerability testing

Third-Party Services

We use reputable third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • RevenueCat - For in-app purchase management Privacy Policy
  • Crashlytics - For crash reporting and stability monitoring Privacy Policy
  • Apple Services - For app distribution and payment processing Privacy Policy

These services collect anonymous usage data only and do not have access to your personal drawings or identifiable information.

Your Privacy Rights

You have full control over your data within the app:

  • Access: View your drawing history in the Gallery section
  • Export: Save drawings as PNG files to your device
  • Delete: Remove drawings individually or reset all progress
  • Synchronization: Enable or disable iCloud syncing anytime

GDPR & CCPA Compliance

We fully comply with international privacy regulations. You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of all data we have about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities

Children's Privacy

Drawing Perfect Shapes is designed for users of all ages but with specific considerations for children:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • All drawing activities are COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) compliant
  • No targeted advertising is displayed in the app
  • Parental controls prevent any unintended in-app purchases
