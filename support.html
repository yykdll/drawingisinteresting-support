Getting Started Guide

Launch the App

Open the app to see the main screen displaying unlocked shape categories. Tap any category to view its drawing challenges.

Start a Challenge

Select an unlocked shape to start the challenge. Carefully observe the example shape displayed on screen before beginning.

Draw Your Shape

Use the brush tool to draw on the canvas. If you make a mistake, use the eraser to correct or the clear button to start over.

Submit & Get Feedback

Tap "Submit" when finished. Get immediate scoring: 80+ to pass, 90+ for 2 stars, 95+ for 3 stars. Unlock the next challenge with 80+ scores.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I improve my score?

Observe example shapes closely, pay attention to lines, proportions, and details. Practice steady drawing to avoid shaky lines. Begin with basic shapes to build fundamental skills before moving to complex drawings.

Why is my drawing scoring low?

The system evaluates three key aspects: line accuracy, shape similarity, and drawing completeness. Low scores typically indicate deviations from the example shape in one or more of these areas. Try to mimic the example more precisely and ensure your drawing fills the canvas appropriately.

How do I unlock more themes?

There are two ways to unlock additional themes: 1) Complete existing challenges to earn points and level up - new themes unlock as you progress through levels. 2) Visit the Settings menu to purchase theme packs for immediate access to additional content.

Where can I change the language?

Language settings are accessible through the Settings screen. From there you can toggle between English and Simplified Chinese. The app will update immediately when you select your preferred language.
